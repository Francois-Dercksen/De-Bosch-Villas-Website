About

Thoughtful design, natural beauty

De Bosch Villas offer an exclusive escape for guests seeking nature within effortless comfort. Every detail of the four individual villas has been shaped around a sense of calm, creating an uninterrupted space to rest and recharge.

Step outside to sweeping views of the Stellenbosch mountains, vibrant vineyards, and grazing horses – all part of the Winelands' rhythm.
